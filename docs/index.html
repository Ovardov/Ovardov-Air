  • What is a mobile boarding pass and how do I use it?

    You can now receive a mobile boarding pass for your Emirates flight on your web-enabled phone or mobile device. In order to use a mobile boarding pass, you need an active internet connection on your mobile device. You can receive a link to your mobile boarding pass either via email or SMS.
